* So, about a month ago, I built [Akshara-Mantapa](https://sanathnu.github.io/Akshara-Mantapa/), a Library of Babel for Kannada. Pretty simple, not too fancy. Was fascinated by the [Library of Babel](https://sites.evergreen.edu/politicalshakespeares/wp-content/uploads/sites/226/2015/12/Borges-The-Library-of-Babel.pdf) (which I re-read again), and wanted to have some fun replicating it.

* There's some really cool extensions I'd like to do for it, like multi-lingual support, maybe even a wiki page or something. Also been thinking about different word frequency distributions to make the output feel more language-like (blog post on that sometime soon), but that's for later.

* Wrote a bunch of blog posts about it. You can start reading [here](https://sanathnu.github.io/blog/web/Ananta-Intro.html). Have fun. Ciao.
